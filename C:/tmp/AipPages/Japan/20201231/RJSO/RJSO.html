3. Lost communication procedures for arrival aircraft under radar navigational guidance
If radio communications with OMINATO GCA are lost for 1 minute in the pattern or 5 seconds (PAR)/15 seconds (ASR) on final approach, squawk Mode A/3 Code 7600 and;
(I)1. Contact MISAWA Radar.
2. If unable, proceed in accordance with visual flight rules.
3. If unable, proceed to BATTL at last assigned altitude or 3,000ft whichever is higher, and execute TACAN A approach.
(II)Procedures other than above will be issued when situation required.
