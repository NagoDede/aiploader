2. Lost communication procedures for arrival aircraft under radar navigational guidance
  If radio communications with Kansai Approach/Radar are lost for 1 minute, squawk Mode A/3 Code 7600 and;
(I)1.Contact Okayama Tower.
2.If unable, proceed in accordance with Visual Flight Rules.
3.If unable,
(1)When the aircraft is at or above 4,000ft,
    proceed to Kibi VOR/DME maintaining the last assigned altitude or 4,000ft whichever is higher
    and execute instrument approach.
(2)When the aircraft is below 4,000ft,
    a. and established on a segment of the Instrument Approach Procedure, execute that Instrument Approach.
    b. and not yet established on a segment of the Instrument Approach Procedure, climb and maintain 4,000ft and
        proceed to Kibi VOR/DME and execute instrument approach.
(II)Procedures other than above will be issued when situation required.
