3. MISSED APCH PROCEDURE FOR PAR/ASR APCH
  • by NDB: Climb to 3000ft on 105° from OH, then turn left within 10NM from OH proceed to OH NDB and hold at 3000ft(4200ft for jet).
  • by TACAN: Climb to 3300ft via TKT R040 to OSABU and hold at 3300ft.

4. Lost Communication Procedures for arrival aircraft under radar navigational guidance
If radio communications with Tokachi GCA are lost for 1 minute in the pattern or 5 seconds(PAR)/15 seconds(ASR) on final approach, squawk Mode A/3 Code 7600 and ;
 1. Contact Tokachi Tower.
 2. If unable, proceed in accordance with visual flight rules.
 3. If unable, execute instrument approach.
