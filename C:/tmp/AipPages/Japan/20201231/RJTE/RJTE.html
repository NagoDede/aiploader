3. Lost communication procedures for arrival aircraft under radar navigational guidance
If radio communications with Tateyama GCA are lost for 1 minute in the pattern, or 5 seconds (PAR)/15 seconds (ASR) on final approach, squawk Mode A/3 Code 7600 and ;
(I)1.Contact Tateyama Tower.
2.If unable, proceed in accordance with Visual Flight Rules.
3.If unable,proceed to TACAN A IAF at last assigned altitude or 2,500 feet whichever is higher and execute TACAN A approach.
(II)Procedures other than above will be issued when situation required.
