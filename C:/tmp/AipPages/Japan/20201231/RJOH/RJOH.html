3. Lost communication procedures for arrival aircraft under radar navigational guidance
If radio communications with Miho Radar/GCA are lost for 1 minute or 5 seconds(PAR)/ 15 seconds(ASR) on final approach, squawk Mode A/3 Code 7600 and;
(I)1.Contact Miho Tower.
2.If unable, proceed in accordance with Visual Flight Rules.
3.If unable, proceed to YVE VOR/DME at last assigned altitude or 4,000ft whichever is higher, and execute instrument approach
(II)Procedures other than above will be issued when situation required.
