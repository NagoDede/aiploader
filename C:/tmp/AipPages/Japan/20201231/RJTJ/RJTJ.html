3.Lost communication procedures for arrival aircraft under radar navigational guidance
  If radio communications with IRUMA GCA are lost for 1 minute in the pattern or 5   seconds (PAR)/15 seconds (ASR) on final approach, squawk Mode A/3 Code 7600 and;
(I)
  1. Contact IRUMA Tower/Yokota Approach.
  2. If unable, proceed in accordance with visual flight rules.
  3. If unable, proceed to TACAN IAF at last assigned altitude or 4,000 feet whichever is higher, and excute TACAN approach.
(II)Procedures other than above will be issued when situation required.
