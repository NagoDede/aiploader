2.Lost communication procedures for arrival aircraft under radar navigational guidance
If radio communications with KANSAI Approach/Radar are lost for 1 minute, squawk Mode A/3 Code 7600 and;
(I)1.Contact TAKAMATSU Tower.
2.If unable, proceed in accordance with visual flight rules,
3.If unable,
(1) When the aircraft is at or above 5,000ft, proceed to KAGAWA VOR/DME maintaining the last assgined altitude or 5,000ft whichever is higher and execute Instrument approach.
(2)When the aircraft is below 5,000ft,
 a.and established on a segment of the Instrument Approach Procedure, execute Instrument Approach.
 b.and not yet established on a segment of the Instrument Approach Procedure, climb and maintain 5,000 feet and proceed to KAGAWA VOR/DME and execute instrument approach.
(II)Procedures other than above will be issued when situation required.
