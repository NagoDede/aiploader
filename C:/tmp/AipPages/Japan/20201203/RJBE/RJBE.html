RJBE AD 2.20  LOCAL TRAFFIC REGULATIONS

1  Airport regulations
1.1 Aircraft operations other than scheduled flights or in an emergency

On use of this airport, aircraft operator is required to obtain the prior permission of the authority.

1.2 Restrictions about the use of auxiliary power units (APU)

When an aircraft is using an aircraft parking stand with fixed electric power facilities, efforts shall be made to avoid using the APU outside the time periods specified below except when specifically acknowledged by the authority as necessary.

(1) 30 minutes prior to the estimated off-block time

(2) The minimum time required for switching over to the fixed electric power facilities or an electric power vehicle for aircraft, after arrival at the parking stand

(3) The minimum time required for aircraft maintenance purposes if needed

Note: Stands 3-6 are equipped with fixed electric power unit.
