RJFS AD 2.21  NOISE ABATEMENT PROCEDURES

Use of Instrument Approach Procedure(IAP) & Standard Instrument Departure(SID) (SEE AD1.1.6.5)
For all aircraft, between 2200JST(1300UTC) and 0000JST(1500UTC), in order to reduce aircraft noise in the vicinity of airport, except in emergency or unavoidable situation, pilots are requested to fly via the following SID and IAP.
  (1) For arrivals : VOR RWY11, RNAV(RNP) RWY11, RNAV(RNP) RWY29, VOR A, VOR C

  (2) For departures : ARIAKE REVERSAL DEPARTURE, SOIGI DEPARTURE
Note) Following procedures should be used only between 2200JST(1300UTC) and 0000JST(1500UTC)
  (1) For arrivals : VOR RWY11
  (2) For departures : ARIAKE REVERSAL DEPARTURE, SOIGI DEPARTURE
