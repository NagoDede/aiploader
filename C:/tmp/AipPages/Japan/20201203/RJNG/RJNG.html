2. Lost communication procedures for arrival aircraft under radar navigational guidance
  If radio communications with Centrair Approach/Radar are lost for 1 minute, squawk Mode A/3 Code 7600 and:
  (I) 1.Contact Gifu Tower.
2.If unable, proceed in accordance with visual flight rules.
3.If unable, proceed to MEIHO IAF at last assigned altitude or 6,000ft whichever is higher and execute TACAN RWY28 approach.
