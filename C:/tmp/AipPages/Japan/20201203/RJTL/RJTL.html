3.Lost Communication Procedures for Arrival aircraft under radar navigational guidance.

 If radio communications with Shimofusa GCA are lost for 1 minute in the pattern or 5 seconds(PAR)/15 seconds(ASR) on final approach, squawk Mode A/3 Code 7600 and;

I
  1. Contact Shimofusa Tower.
  2. If unable,proceed in accordance with visual flight rules.
  3. If unable,proceed to TOHNE at last assigned altitude or 3000ft whichever is higher, and execute instrument approach.
II

Procedures other than above will be issued when situation required.
